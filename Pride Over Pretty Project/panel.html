Panel

Here, we post the interviews held with women from different backgrounds and careers. We explore the experiences that led them to their optimal career choices and successes. Our panels feature a diverse set of women from a varied set of occupations to provide an outlet for young girls to reach and view. Through these panels, we hope to inspire young girls who hope to pursue similar careers in the future.

PANEL #1

NITHYA MANI

TECHNICAL PROGRAM MANAGER

Our first panel highlights the story of Nithya Mani, a technical program manager at Tesla, Amsterdam. She speaks on her experiences as a student and her journey to her current occupation. Learn more about what it means to work as a technical program manager and receive the advice she provides for any girls hoping to pursue a similar career.
