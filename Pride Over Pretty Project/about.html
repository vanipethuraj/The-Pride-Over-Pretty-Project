Who We Are

Two girls, apprehensively excited about their futures as adults, decided to create the Pride over Pretty Project to build a platform where women from a diverse set of workplaces could share their stories and struggles. All our proceeds go directly to funding causes which fight for the advancement of women in the workplace as well as the education of girls in underprivileged countries.

VANI PETHURAJ

CO-FOUNDER

Vani Pethuraj is a rising high-school junior who feels passionate about technology and computer science. She has never really found herself interested in social change issues prior to this project but has recently become exposed to the prevalent and ongoing gender bias problems in the workplace. Vani strongly believes that this difficulty must be addressed and receive awareness.

HARITHA RAJASEKAR

CO-FOUNDER

Haritha Rajasekar is a rising high-school junior who sees a future in advocacy and human rights. Through this project, she wishes to acknowledge the struggles faced by women in a diverse set of fields while also building ideas to combat these issues. Haritha hopes this project will enable young girls everywhere to gain optimism regarding their future careers, nurturing and encouraging young minds.
